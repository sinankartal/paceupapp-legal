Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a workout?

Go to the "Today" tab and tap "Start Empty Workout". You can then add exercises and track your sets.

Can I create custom exercises?

Yes! Go to the "Exercises" tab, tap the "+" button, and fill in the exercise details.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely using Supabase with industry-standard security measures. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I use the app offline?

Currently, RepWise requires an internet connection to sync your data. Offline support is planned for future updates.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data. This action cannot be undone.

I forgot my password / can't sign in

RepWise uses 6-digit verification code (OTP) authentication - you don't need a password! Simply enter your email on the sign-in screen, and we'll send you a secure 6-digit code to access your account.

The verification code isn't working

Make sure to:

  • Enter the code exactly as it appears in your email
  • Request a new code if the current one has expired (codes expire after 10 minutes)
  • Check your spam folder if you don't see the email

What languages are supported?

RepWise is currently available in English, Turkish (Türkçe), and Dutch (Nederlands). You can change your preferred language anytime in the Settings tab.

How do I change my email address?

Currently, email addresses cannot be changed. If you need to use a different email, you can create a new account and export your data from the old one.

Does the app cost money?

RepWise is currently free to use with no hidden costs or subscriptions.

Getting Started

New to RepWise? Here's a quick guide:

  1. Create an Account: Enter your email and input the 6-digit verification code sent to you
  2. Start Your First Workout: Go to "Today" tab and tap "Start Empty Workout"
  3. Add Exercises: Tap "Add Exercise" and select from the library or create custom ones
  4. Track Sets: Enter weight and reps for each set, then mark as complete
  5. Finish: Tap "Finish" when done to save your workout
  6. View History: Check the "History" tab to see all your past workouts
