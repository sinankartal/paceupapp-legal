Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 12, 2025

PaceUp ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PaceUp mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our Service, in accordance with GDPR regulations.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication purposes.
  • Workout Data: Exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, and workout history.
  • Usage Data: App interactions and preferences (e.g., theme selection).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service.
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts.
  • Store and sync your workout data across devices.
  • Improve and personalize your experience.
  • Send service-related notifications.

Legal Basis for Processing:

We process your data based on your consent and to fulfill our contract with you (providing the app and its features). You may withdraw your consent at any time.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted backend provider. Measures include:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Row-level security policies
  • Regular security updates

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Supabase for hosting and authentication
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and data permanently
  • Correct your account information
  • Withdraw consent for any non-essential processing

6. Data Transfers

Some data may be processed or stored outside the European Economic Area (EEA). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. After deletion, all personal data and workout history are permanently removed.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Cookies and Analytics

If any tracking or analytics services are used, we will provide transparency and obtain consent where required.

10. Complaints

You may lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority if you believe your data has been mishandled.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication and data storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo: For app development and over-the-air updates (Privacy Policy)
